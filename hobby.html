MY CURRENT HOBBY

Hiking at Bukit H

  • I would like to share my experience of hiking for the first time. I started hiking because of the influence of my friend who is very passionate about hiking. She took me hiking when we were at university. He took us hiking in Bukit H, Sungai Petani, Kedah during the holidays. This first experience was so much fun for me as we were able to see the beautiful sunrise.


Hiking at Bukit Bankong

  • Also, I was hiking with my sister in Bukit Bankong, Chemor, Ipoh, Perak when I spent the night at her house. In addition to being able to keep the body healthy, we can also see the beautiful view of the city of Ipoh from the top of the hill.
